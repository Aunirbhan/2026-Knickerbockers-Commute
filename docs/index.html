MTA Ridership Around Madison Square Garden — 2026 Knicks Playoff Run

Every bubble below is a subway station complex next to Madison Square Garden — the two 34 St–Penn Station complexes and 34 St–Herald Sq — and every frame is one hour between April 10 and June 12, 2026, the Knicks' playoff run. Bubbles grow with hourly ridership; days are colored by whether the Knicks played at home (orange-red), away (gold), or not at all (blue). Away games are the control: the same fans are watching, but nobody is walking out of MSG into the subway, so if home nights surge and comparable away and no-game nights don't, the extra riders are the arena crowd. Scrub to a home game's post-game hours — say Sat Apr 18 around 9 PM, or Tue May 19 at 11 PM — and compare the same hours one day later.

Data: MTA Subway Hourly Ridership via data.ny.gov, dataset 5wq4-mkjj. Ridership values are MTA estimates at hourly granularity. Code is MIT licensed; this writeup is CC-BY 4.0.
